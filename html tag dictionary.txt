a: "anchor" aka link, makes a link clickable
atribbutes: href
example: <a href="https://www.discordapp.com">Discord</a>

h1: Header tag, makes the text large and puts it on a separate line. good for making titles or headings.
attributes: none
example: <h1>Howard Qiu</h1>

h6: Header tag, like h1, but the text is smaller. header tages range from h1 to h6, h1 being largest and h6 being smallest. good for subheadings.
attributes: none
example: <h6>Things I Like</h6>

img: Attatches an image
attributes: src
example: <img src="bread.png">

li: makes a list, can only be used alongside ol or ul
attributes: none
example: <li>Rainbow 6 Siege</li>

ol: makes an ordered list, must be used alongside li
attributes: none
example: <ol><li>Traffic Jams</li><ol>

p: makes a paragraph/block of text
attributes: none
example: <p> aaaaaaaa </p>

ul: makes a bullet list, must be ised alongside li
attributes: none
example: <ul><li>Rainbow 6 Siege</li><ul>
